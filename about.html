About Ambassador Mulugeta Kelil

Diplomatic Excellence

Ambassador Mulugeta Kelil brings over two decades of diplomatic and business expertise to international consulting. As a former Ethiopian Ambassador to South Africa and current Diplomat at the Consulate General of Ethiopia in Los Angeles, he offers unique insights into international relations and business development.

Academic Background

With a Master's degree in Economics from Addis Ababa University and experience as both a lecturer and business executive, Ambassador Kelil combines academic excellence with practical leadership skills to deliver comprehensive solutions for his clients.

Business Acumen

His unique combination of diplomatic service and private sector experience as CEO of Alsam PLC provides a comprehensive understanding of both government relations and business operations, enabling him to bridge the gap between public and private sectors effectively.

Global Perspective

Having served in diplomatic missions across multiple continents and worked with diverse international organizations, Ambassador Kelil brings a truly global perspective to every consulting engagement, helping clients navigate the complexities of international business and diplomatic relations.
