About Ambassador Mulugeta Kelil

Ambassador Mulugeta Kelil brings over two decades of diplomatic and business expertise to international consulting. As a former Ethiopian Ambassador to South Africa and current Diplomat at the Consulate General of Ethiopia in Los Angeles, he offers unique insights into international relations and business development.

With a Master's degree in Economics from Addis Ababa University and experience as both a lecturer and business executive, Ambassador Kelil combines academic excellence with practical leadership skills to deliver comprehensive solutions for his clients.
